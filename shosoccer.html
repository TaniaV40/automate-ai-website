ShoSoccer

From manual side hustle to scalable coaching business

How operational automation unlocked growth, eliminated admin bottlenecks, and turned coaching time into revenue.

52% increase in annual revenue

179% growth in lead database

100% upfront payment collection

The starting point

ShoSoccer is a grassroots football coaching business built around passion, community, and high-quality training sessions for young players.

The business relied heavily on manual coordination — bookings, payments, and communications were handled through messages and spreadsheets.

The problem wasn’t coaching quality. It was operational invisibility.

High expertise. Zero systems.

Manual booking confirmations

Spreadsheet-based admin

Chasing payments after sessions

No visibility on leads or demand

Impact:

Evenings and weekends were consumed by admin. Growth was capped by available hours. Revenue leaked through late or missed payments.

Building an operating system for growth

Instead of patching individual problems, Series Media designed a complete operational system that connected booking, payments, communications, and reporting into one simple flow.

Automated booking and payment flow

Centralised CRM with smart segmentation

Automated email and reminder sequences

AI-assisted lead nurturing

Real-time reporting and visibility

Measured impact

Revenue increased from £58k to £89.5k

Lead database grew from 78 to 218

Bad debt eliminated entirely

12+ hours saved per week

The business moved from reactive admin to proactive growth planning.
