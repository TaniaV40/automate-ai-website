## Who we are
Series Media is an AI automation agency based in Swansea, Wales, UK. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data when you interact with our website and services.

## What data we collect
- Contact details you submit via forms, including name and email address.
- Basic message content you provide in enquiries.
- Technical data such as browser type and IP address for security and analytics.

## How we use your data
- To respond to enquiries and provide requested information.
- To deliver services and manage our client relationship.
- To improve our website, security, and service delivery.

## Legal basis for processing (UK GDPR)
- Consent: when you submit a form or request information.
- Contract: to deliver services you request or purchase.
- Legitimate interests: to improve our services and protect our business.

## How we share data
We do not sell your data. We may share it with trusted service providers who process data on our behalf (for example, email and hosting providers). These providers are required to keep your data secure and use it only for the services they provide to us.

## International transfers
If data is transferred outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in line with UK GDPR requirements.

## Data retention
We keep personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy or to meet legal obligations.

## Your rights
- Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
- Object to or restrict processing in certain circumstances.
- Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
- Complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

## Contact us
For privacy questions or requests, contact us at tania@seriesmedia.net.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.
